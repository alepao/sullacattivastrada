ABOUT US

"Sulla cattiva strada" is an original music project creating fusion between acoustic guitar, violin/viola and voice. An unusual mix of classical, modern and retro music, we play both popular and lessknown songs.

Maestro Lorenzo Bozzi plays violino and viola and Laura Menchetti plays guitars and sings, together we created an elegant and refined mood.
